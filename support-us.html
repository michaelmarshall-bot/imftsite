    Donate

    The Ibbotson Memorial Field is a voluntary organisation and has limited funds. By donating here you are supporting the maintenance and upkeep of the field. All donations are very gratefully received, thank you for your support.

    Support Us

    The Ibbotson Memorial Field Trust is a voluntary organisation and works alongside Cricket, Tennis, Bowls, the Village Hall, and Bradfield in Bloom to maintain the field and its associated activities.

    We are always keen to welcome anyone who may wish to help. If you have some spare time and would like to get involved please get in touch by emailing:
    administrator@ibbotsonmemorialfield.com
    or contact the individual activity groups.
